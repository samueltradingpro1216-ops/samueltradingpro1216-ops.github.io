Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

TikTok Auto ("the App") is a content scheduling and publishing tool that integrates with the TikTok API. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the service:

  • TikTok account basic information (username, user ID) — used to authenticate and publish content on your behalf.
  • Video content you choose to upload and schedule.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf via OAuth 2.0.
  • Publish or schedule video content to your TikTok account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is transmitted to TikTok's API as required to fulfill the publishing function.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored locally and are never sent to any third-party server other than TikTok. You may revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by revoking the app's permissions directly on TikTok.

7. Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us via the GitHub repository.
